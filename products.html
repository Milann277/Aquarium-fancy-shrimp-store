(10x) Crystal Red Shrimp

$19.90

Description: The Crystal Red Shrimp is a beautiful, high-quality shrimp variety known for its striking red and white pattern. It thrives in well-maintained aquariums with a steady pH level and clean, soft water.

Care Needs: Maintain a water temperature between 72°F and 78°F (22°C - 26°C), with a pH between 6.5 and 7.5. Ensure proper filtration and regular water changes to keep these shrimp healthy.

(10x) Blue Dream Shrimp

$19.90

Description: The Blue Dream Shrimp is known for its vibrant blue coloration, which can vary in intensity depending on water conditions. It's an active shrimp that enjoys a well-planted tank.

Care Needs: These shrimp do well in a temperature range of 70°F to 78°F (21°C - 25°C) with a pH level between 6.5 and 7.5. Regular water changes and a moderate current are beneficial for them.

(10x) Golden Bee Shrimp

$19.90

Description: The Golden Bee Shrimp has a striking yellow body with black markings. They are highly sought after for their appearance and ease of care.

Care Needs: These shrimp thrive in temperatures between 68°F and 78°F (20°C - 26°C) and a pH level of 6.5 to 7.5. They prefer soft, slightly acidic water and benefit from frequent water changes.

(10x) Cherry Shrimp

$14.90

Description: Cherry Shrimp are known for their bright red coloration and are one of the most popular freshwater shrimp in the hobby. They are easy to care for and adapt well to various water conditions.

Care Needs: These shrimp do well in temperatures between 65°F and 75°F (18°C - 24°C) with a pH between 6.5 and 7.5. Provide them with live plants and hiding spots to thrive.
