Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to care for my shrimp?

To ensure the best care for your shrimp, maintain clean water, appropriate temperature, and avoid sudden changes in water conditions. Our shrimp thrive in well-maintained aquariums with stable water parameters.

Can I order shrimp for my aquarium online?

Yes! You can order shrimp directly from our website, and we will ship them to your door with care instructions to ensure they arrive safely.

Are your shrimp suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! We offer shrimp for both beginners and advanced aquarium enthusiasts. Our team is available to provide guidance and recommendations based on your experience level.
